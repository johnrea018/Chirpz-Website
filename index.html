Our story
Quiet gear for loud swings.

Chirpz was born from the feeling that most golf gear is either too loud or too boring. We wanted something in between – clean, modern gloves with just enough personality to feel like yours, not a walking billboard for someone else’s logo.

The rule is simple: if it doesn’t help your swing or your confidence on the tee, it doesn’t make it onto the glove.

Subtle branding
Modern color stories
Golfer-designed details
Built to feel unreal
Thin, premium-feeling materials chosen to move with your hand, not fight it. The goal is simple: forget it’s there until you take it off and realize how locked-in you felt.
Made for modern fits
Your glove shouldn’t clash with your outfit. Chirpz colorways are designed to sit cleanly with today’s on-course styles – from neutral to bold.
