Our Story

Chirpz began as a simple idea: premium golf gloves with modern style and a little personality. Built for the golfer who likes to chirp, joke around, and play with confidence.

Founded by John Rea, Chirpz is rooted in the belief that golf gear can be clean, classy, and still have character. Every glove is designed to feel great and bring subtle style to your game—without sacrificing performance.
