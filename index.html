Our story

Built by a golfer who got tired of loud gear.

Chirpz started with one simple idea: your glove shouldn’t look like a billboard. It should feel unreal, last longer than a single hot round, and quietly match the rest of your fit. No giant cartoon logos, no weird plastics — just gear that plays as clean as it looks.

“Let the gear do the talking” isn’t just a tagline. It’s the filter for every design decision we make.

Featherweight feel
Thin, premium-feeling material designed to move with your hand, not fight against it.
Locked-in grip
Built to keep the club stable without feeling sticky or bulky.
Modern minimal
Subtle branding, clean colorways, and artwork that doesn’t fight your outfit.
